From Factory to Market

  1. Manufacturer makes the guns and requests gun registration
  2. Government registers the guns and commits the data
  3. Manufacturer sends guns to distributer and requests status change
  4. Government save the changes and commits the data
  5. Guns are sold by distributer and request is sent to Government for changes
  6. Government save the changes and commits the data

  2. Government performs background checks and issue permits and commits
