Money flow in CodexArma

CodexArma needs to be implemented at an administrative level. So, it will be presented to the government as a service that is provided and maintained by us on a contractual basis. This will be our primary revenue stream. CodexArma also creates a framework that allows firearms manufacturers to scrutinize the demographics to whom their products are sold and how much of each product is sold, thus allowing them access to valuable market data from reliable sources. Manufacturers who wish to obtain this data will be charged for it. This will be the secondary revenue stream. (Note: this is an additional feature that manufacturers may opt to avail. They do not pay for providing data to the blockchain.) Another potential source of revenue could be insurance companies that wish to use the blockchain for data collection on gun owners.
